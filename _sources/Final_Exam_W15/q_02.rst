   Assume you have two coins: a **fair coin** and a **biased coin**. The **fair coin** lands on heads 50% of the time and lands on tails the other 50% of the time. The **biased coin** and lands on heads 75% of the time and tails 25% of the time. Which coin provides more information when you learn the outcome of a flip?
